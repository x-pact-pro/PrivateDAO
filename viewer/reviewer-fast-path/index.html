Reviewer Fast Path

This is the shortest high-signal route for a judge, auditor, technical reviewer, or curious operator.

The goal is simple: in under two minutes, the reviewer should understand that PrivateDAO is not a pile of disconnected experiments. It is one product where governance, confidential operations, telemetry, and trust reinforce each other.

Two-Minute Review

  • https://privatedao.org/start/
  • This shows the wallet-first governance path as a normal-user product entry, not only a protocol demo.
  1. Open the live product shell:
  • https://privatedao.org/security/
  • This shows how privacy and confidential treasury motion are expressed as usable workflows, not only cryptographic vocabulary.
  1. Open the confidential operations surface:
  • https://privatedao.org/services/
  • This shows governed treasury motion, payout planning, route context, and execution framing inside one operating lane.
  1. Open the services surface:
  • https://privatedao.org/trust/
  • This shows the review posture: hardening, evidence, custody truth, and launch discipline.
  1. Open the trust surface:
  • https://privatedao.org/judge/
  • This shows the shortest reviewer-facing path into proposal lifecycle, Devnet signatures, and the agentic micropayment rail.
  1. Open the judge route:
  • https://privatedao.org/proof/?judge=1
  • This shows that the product is paired with reviewer-readable evidence rather than unsupported claims.
  1. Open the proof surface:

Why this route works

This path compresses the whole thesis into one readable sequence:

  • start proves the governance product is real
  • security proves privacy is part of the workflow
  • services proves treasury and payment motion are operational, not decorative
  • trust proves the team cares about reviewability and launch discipline
  • judge proves that captured Devnet execution is easy to inspect
  • proof proves the claims are tied to evidence
