Product Alignment Requirements 2026

This document is the canonical public alignment note for PrivateDAO during the current external review cycle.

It is intentionally strict:

  • it separates verified product truth from roadmap inference
  • it explains technical alignment without exposing sponsor tactics
  • it does not overclaim integrations, external audits, encrypted AI, or mainnet readiness

Current product truth

PrivateDAO already provides, truthfully:

  • private DAO governance on Solana Devnet
  • wallet-first governance flow in the public web app
  • reviewer-safe proof, runtime evidence, and trust packets
  • confidential payout and treasury motion rehearsal backed by FHE / REFHE, ZK, MagicBlock, and Fast RPC
  • telemetry, diagnostics, and read-oriented infrastructure surfaces

PrivateDAO is being advanced toward honest mainnet-ready status through an active closure program. The current operating milestones are:

  1. external audit completion
  2. authority multisig and custody hardening closure
  3. production monitoring and alerting closure
  4. full real-device wallet runtime matrix
  5. source-verifiable settlement receipts for the confidential operations corridor
  6. final mainnet cutover ceremony

Primary blocker sources:

  • docs/mainnet-blockers.json
  • docs/mainnet-go-live-checklist.md

Evaluation method

For this public note:

  • verified alignment means “mapped to a shipped surface or an explicit next milestone already present in the repo”
  • public fit means “described in technical and product terms that a reviewer can verify”
  • private submission tactics stay outside the repo

Highest-value track-to-service mapping

PrivateDAO core

Best public role:

  • the governance and treasury product shell
  • the live product lane for reviewers and operators

Why:

  • already live
  • already wallet-first
  • already reviewer-readable

Read API and runtime infrastructure

Best public role:

  • telemetry
  • diagnostics
  • export
  • operator trust

Why:

  • this is the clearest infrastructure lane already visible in the product

Payments and treasury operations

Best public role:

  • governed treasury motion
  • payout funding
  • rebalance planning
  • business-readable execution context

Why:

  • the product already has the right treasury and operational base
  • the next uplift is a cleaner swap, payout, or commerce lane, not a new product category

Confidential operations corridor

Best public role:

  • private governance tied to real treasury workflows

Why:

  • it connects cryptography to usable organizational behavior

Trust and hardening corridor

Best public role:

  • reviewability
  • hardening discipline
  • launch seriousness

Why:

  • reviewers and supporters both need honest operational maturity, not only features

What we should present with precision

  • external audit as an active hardening milestone
  • mainnet production readiness as the next major operating threshold
  • encrypted AI as a future-ready lane until the operator surface is fully shipped
  • partner-native dashboards or publications only at the level currently evidenced
  • same-domain hosted backend cutover as an execution milestone until the real cutover happens

Highest-value implementation priorities

  1. keep the governance workspace easier to understand and complete
  2. keep the confidential operations lane easier to explain to both reviewers and normal users
  3. keep telemetry and exports more legible for infrastructure-minded reviewers
  4. keep the governed treasury route more executable and operator-readable
  5. keep trust and hardening materials aligned as one serious review path

Immediate product thesis

If PrivateDAO wants first-place probability rather than broad participation optics during the current review cycle, the strongest public priority stack is:

  1. one coherent product story
  2. stronger privacy and confidential-operations usability
  3. stronger telemetry and infrastructure readability
  4. stronger treasury motion and payments execution planning
  5. stronger audit-readiness and trust packaging

Strategic posture

PrivateDAO is being built with the ambition to earn first-place outcomes through serious product work, visible operating discipline, and reviewer-readable trust.

The team is working continuously to turn every technical layer into something stronger, easier to explain, and more useful to the ecosystem. The goal is not only to perform well during the current cycle, but to convert support into a longer mission: helping shape stronger governance, stronger operational safety, and stronger privacy-aware infrastructure for the ecosystem over time.
