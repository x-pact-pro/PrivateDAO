Weekly Product Update #2

Goal

This is the second weekly product update video for the live PrivateDAO build.

It should be:

  • under 60 seconds
  • narrated, not silent
  • product-first
  • easy for a non-technical judge to follow
  • explicit that the live path runs from wallet connection to private vote lifecycle

This is not the long-form overview video and not the final pitch.

Recommended Upload Title

Week 2 - PrivateDAO Product Update - Guided Governance And Agentic Treasury Rail

Recommended Video Description

PrivateDAO is building public-good governance infrastructure on Solana for private voting, safer treasury execution, and reviewer-visible runtime proof. Week 2 shows the live wallet-first product path becoming easier for normal users and judges to follow: create a proposal, commit a private vote, reveal later, execute through the same Devnet app, then inspect the new Agentic Treasury Micropayment Rail with real Devnet transactions and screenshots. The focus this week is clarity, usability, and proof visibility inside one real product surface.

60-Second Spoken Script

Week two is where PrivateDAO becomes easier for normal users to understand and easier for judges to verify.

The wallet-connected product now guides proposal creation, private voting, reveal, and execution without asking people to think like protocol engineers.

The core wedge stays the same: commit now, reveal later, and execute with clearer operational boundaries.

What changed is product clarity and proof density.

Learn, start, and judge now reinforce the same live governance story, and the new Agentic Treasury Micropayment Rail shows that one approved flow can create many real Devnet settlements.

That means the reviewer can understand the flow faster, and the user can move through the same product with less friction while still inspecting hashes, logs, and screenshots.

From here, the next step is stable settlement, stronger trust surfaces, and broader runtime proof on top of the same working Devnet product.
